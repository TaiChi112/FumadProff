### 3. ‡∏Ñ‡∏≥‡∏ô‡∏ß‡∏ì Euclidean ‡πÅ‡∏•‡∏∞ Manhattan ‡∏à‡∏≤‡∏Å‡∏à‡∏∏‡∏î (1,2,0,5) ‡πÑ‡∏õ (-2,6,3,-1)

**‡∏Å‡∏≥‡∏´‡∏ô‡∏î‡πÉ‡∏´‡πâ:** X = (1, 2, 0, 5) ‡πÅ‡∏•‡∏∞ Y = (-2, 6, 3, -1)

### 3.1 Euclidean Distance ($L_2$ Norm)

**‡∏™‡∏π‡∏ï‡∏£:** $\sqrt{\sum (x_i - y_i)^2}$
{/* d = ‚àöŒ£(x·µ¢ - y·µ¢)¬≤  */}

**‡∏Å‡∏≤‡∏£‡∏Ñ‡∏≥‡∏ô‡∏ß‡∏ì:**

$$
\begin{aligned}
d(X,Y) &= \sqrt{(1 - (-2))^2 + (2 - 6)^2 + (0 - 3)^2 + (5 - (-1))^2} \\
&= \sqrt{(3)^2 + (-4)^2 + (-3)^2 + (6)^2} \\
&= \sqrt{9 + 16 + 9 + 36} \\
&= \sqrt{70} \\
&\approx \mathbf{8.366}
\end{aligned}
$$

### 3.2 Manhattan Distance (($L_1$ Norm))

**‡∏™‡∏π‡∏ï‡∏£:** $\sum |x_i - y_i|$
{/* d = Œ£|x·µ¢ - y·µ¢|  */}

**‡∏Å‡∏≤‡∏£‡∏Ñ‡∏≥‡∏ô‡∏ß‡∏ì:**

$$
\begin{aligned}
d(X,Y) &= |1 - (-2)| + |2 - 6| + |0 - 3| + |5 - (-1)| \\
&= |3| + |-4| + |-3| + |6| \\
&= 3 + 4 + 3 + 6 \\
&= \mathbf{16}
\end{aligned}
$$
